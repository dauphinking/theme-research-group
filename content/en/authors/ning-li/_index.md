Ning Li is a Tenured Professor at the School of Automation and Sensing, Shanghai Jiao Tong University. Her research focuses on distributed optimization and control, prognostic and health management, active perception and intelligent decision-making of unmanned systems, and artificial intelligence.

## Professional Experience

* **Associate Professor/Professor:** School of Electronic, Information and Electrical Engineering, Shanghai Jiao Tong University (Dec 2005–Present)
* **Visiting Scholar:** Massachusetts Institute of Technology (Feb–May 2009)
* **Visiting Research Fellow:** National University of Singapore (Jul–Oct 2011)
* **Lecturer/Associate Professor:** East China University of Science and Technology (Apr 2002–Dec 2005)

## Academic Service

* Associate Editor, *Transactions of the Institute of Measurement and Control*
* Member, Big Data Committee, Chinese Association of Automation
* Member, Predictive Control and Intelligent Decision-making Committee, Chinese Association of Automation
* Member, Cloud Control and Decision-making Committee, Chinese Association of Command and Control
* Member, Artificial Intelligence Simulation Technology Committee, Chinese Society of Simulation
* Council Member, Shanghai Automation Society
