Youren Chen is a Senior Laboratory Engineer at the School of Automation and Sensing, Shanghai Jiao Tong University, and Executive Vice Dean of the SJTU Ningbo Artificial Intelligence Institute. His research areas include intelligent transportation systems, information fusion, and green energy. He also contributes to research-industry collaboration and talent development in artificial intelligence and the industrial internet.

Office: Room 2-424, Telecom Building, Shanghai Jiao Tong University.
