      title: From Research to Deployable Control Systems
      subtitle: Model predictive control, industrial software, and edge deployment
      text: |
        The Complex Systems Control Laboratory (CSC Lab) at Shanghai Jiao Tong University conducts research in model predictive control (MPC), data-driven control, real-time optimization, autonomous robotics, and intelligent transportation. Engineers and industry partners connect this research with industrial software, edge controllers, PLC/DCS integration, and site delivery.

        [Prof. Dewei Li](/en/author/dewei-li/) directs the laboratory. [Bihui Jin](/en/author/bihui-jin/) works on control algorithms, edge systems, and industrial software, and serves as technology lead at Shanghai Bidi Data Technology.

  - block: markdown
    content:
      title: QD-APC and Shanghai Bidi Data Technology
      subtitle: A documented technology-transfer path
      text: |
        **QD-APC** is an engineering framework for advanced process control. It covers model identification, predictive control, constrained optimization, condition monitoring, control-authority switching, and performance assessment.

        * **CSC Lab**: MPC, data-driven control, online optimization, and algorithm validation
        * **Shanghai Bidi Data Technology**: industrial software, edge controllers, OPC UA/Modbus/PROFINET/MQTT integration, site deployment, and operations
        * **Engineering lead**: [Bihui Jin](/en/author/bihui-jin/)

        [View QD-APC Advanced Process Control Software →](/en/project/advanced-apc-software-system/)

  - block: markdown
    content:
      title: Water and Pumping Control
      text: |
        CSC Lab's public research foundation includes a National Natural Science Foundation key project on modeling, optimal operation, and control of urban drainage networks, as well as an industry project on whole-process optimization for smart water plants. Engineering work extends this foundation to dosing, aeration, pump groups, and pumping-station scheduling.

  - block: markdown
    content:
      title: Process and Coal-Chemical Control
      text: |
        CSC Lab leads a National Key R&D Program project on key technologies and equipment for full closed-loop control of coal-conversion processes. This research project is part of the laboratory's public technical foundation in process control.

        The national research project is not the same as the QD-APC commercial product, and this page does not state that Shanghai Bidi Data Technology is a project participant. Research projects, intellectual property, commercial products, and customer engagements are managed by their respective entities.
