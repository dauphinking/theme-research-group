## Jing Wu speaks at the YAC 2026 Outstanding Young Talents Forum

The 41st Chinese Association of Automation Youth Academic Annual Conference (YAC 2026) was held in Changsha from May 8 to 10, 2026. Researcher Jing Wu spoke at Outstanding Young Talents Forum II on “Security Control and State Estimation for Power Cyber-Physical Systems: From Existing Methods to Resilience Perspectives.”

The event and speaker information are available from the Chinese Association of Automation Youth Committee's [YAC 2026 conference page](https://www.caayac.org.cn/yac2026/) and [forum speaker list](https://www.caayac.org.cn/yxqn22036/).

## Aoyun Ma named an Outstanding Reviewer

In June 2026, the journals *Control Theory and Applications* and *Control Theory and Technology* announced their 2025 annual awards. Assistant Researcher Aoyun Ma was named a 2025 Outstanding Reviewer for *Control Theory and Technology*.

The full list is available in the journals' [2025 annual awards announcement](https://blog.sciencenet.cn/blog-3635716-1538704.html).
