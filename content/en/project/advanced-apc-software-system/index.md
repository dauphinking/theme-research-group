QD-APC addresses closed-loop optimization in industrial operations. It connects CSC Lab research in model predictive control (MPC), data-driven control, and real-time optimization with the edge controllers, industrial connectivity, and project delivery capabilities of Shanghai Bidi Data Technology.

**Technology and engineering roles:**

* CSC Lab: predictive control, data-driven modeling, online optimization, and control algorithms
* Shanghai Bidi Data Technology: industrial software, edge controllers, protocol integration, site deployment, and operations
* Engineering lead: [Bihui Jin](/en/author/bihui-jin/)

**Key Features:**

* Model predictive control (MPC) integrated with data-driven modeling
* Customizable development for industrial applications
* Constrained optimization and adaptive setpoints for multiple control loops
* OPC UA, Modbus, PROFINET, MQTT, and other common interfaces
* Edge deployment, PLC/DCS integration, and safe control-authority switching

**Applications:**

* Water: dosing, aeration, pump groups, and pumping-station optimization
* Process industries: multivariable temperature, pressure, flow, and energy control
* Energy and manufacturing: compressed-air stations, thermal management, power generation, and production processes
